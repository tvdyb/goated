# Audit C — Phase 07 Claim Index: Kalshi Weekly Soybean Contract Structure

## 1. Source artifact summary

`research/phase_07_kalshi_contract_structure.md` dissects the legal, mechanical, and technological layers of KalshiEX LLC's weekly soybean event contract series `KXSOYBEANW`, anchored on the sample market ticker `kxsoybeanw-26apr2417` (Event `KXSOYBEANW-26APR24`, bucket index `17`). It walks through the four-level Series → Event → Market → Yes/No identifier hierarchy, the Rule 40.2(a) self-certification template that hosts per-contract Terms and Conditions in Appendix A, the rulebook chapters governing bucket specifications (13.1), order matching and priority (5.9, 5.10), book depth (5.13), the $0.20 No Cancellation Range (5.11), position limits expressed as max-loss dollar caps (5.19), settlement (6.3) and outcome review (7.1, 7.2), the cent-granular tick and $0.01–$0.99 price band, the Rule 4 Designated Market Maker program, the closed-form `ceil(0.07 · P(1−P) · 100)/100` taker fee with 25%-of-taker maker rebate, the REST + WebSocket API surface signed with RSA-PSS SHA-256 over `{ts}{method}{path}`, the tiered leaky-bucket rate limits, and Klear-cleared cash-collateralized settlement. The artifact explicitly flags ten unresolved ambiguities — chiefly the exact CBOT reference contract month and settlement-snapshot time, the per-contract position limit, any commodity-specific fee surcharge, and the live bucket grid for the sample event — because `kalshi.com` returned HTTP 429 throughout the research window and no `KXSOYBEANW` Appendix A had yet been indexed in the CFTC SIRT portal.
