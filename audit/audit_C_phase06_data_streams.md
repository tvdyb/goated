# Audit C — Phase 06: Data Streams for Pricing the Soybean Complex

## 1. Source Scope Summary

`research/phase_06_data_streams.md` is a primary-source catalog of every materially useful data stream for pricing CBOT soybean complex futures (ZS/ZM/ZL) on intraday through multi-week horizons. It documents 40+ distinct streams across three temporal regimes — microsecond microstructure (CME MDP 3.0), seconds-to-minutes scheduled fundamentals and weather (USDA, NOAA, ECMWF), and days-to-weeks alt-data (logistics, satellite, positioning). For each stream the source records publisher, exact product, native format, access method, publication latency, update cadence, cost tier, and the explicit distinction between feeds that drive continuous quoting versus feeds that drive slower fair-value updates. Geographic coverage spans U.S. (USDA/CFTC/USACE), South America (CONAB/SECEX/BCBA/BCR), China (GACC/MARA/Sinograin), and global meteorology (NOAA/ECMWF/NASA/Copernicus), with a commercial alt-data layer (Kpler, MarineTraffic, Descartes, Gro, Planet, Barchart, DTN). Each section closes with a "Latency vs. value for market making" practitioner-lore framing.

## 3. What This File Does NOT Claim

The source is a stream catalog with practitioner-lore framing and deliberately stops short of several adjacent topics that one might expect:

- **No specific futures contract specifications.** The file names ZS/ZM/ZL but does not record tick size, contract size, point value, last-trading-day rules, or first-notice-day mechanics for the underlying futures.
- **No formula-level pricing models.** It says CVOL is "deep OTM-weighted" without giving the kernel; it cites Rule 813 VWAP without specifying the settlement window length; it does not give a fair-value or basis-model formulation.
- **No backtest results, signal performance metrics, or empirical betas.** The phrase "realized sigma × empirical-beta" is used (C06-87) but no calibrated value is given.
- **No specific feed-handler implementations or code references.** SBE/FIX 5.0 SP2 and GLBX.MDP3 are named but no library, parser, or schema-version pin appears.
- **No volume/throughput numbers** for any non-CME stream — only soybean futures volume/OI orders of magnitude (C06-13) appear.
- **No coverage of historical Quick Stats series limits beyond the 50,000-row request cap** (C06-24, C06-26).
- **No mention of CFTC Traders-in-Financial-Futures (TFF) report**, of any options-on-futures positioning equivalent to COT, or of any non-U.S. positioning data.
- **No vendor-tier-equivalent coverage** for European or Asian alt-data publishers beyond Kpler (which subsumes MarineTraffic).
- **No internal data-quality, schema-evolution, or change-management guidance** for any of the streams; "near-real-time" and "EOD" are used without defining failure modes or staleness alerts.
- **No exchange-fee, license-fee, or legal-redistribution rules** are quoted at the line-item level — cost tiers are bucketed, not enumerated.
